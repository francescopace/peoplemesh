# PeopleMesh — Business Model

## Positioning

PeopleMesh is an open-source, AI-powered discovery platform for internal company use.
It helps employees find colleagues, internal opportunities, communities, projects, and initiatives through semantic search.

The product starts with a practical intranet problem: reducing time-to-discover inside large organizations.

## Open-Source-First Principles

1. **Core product stays open:** Core search and matching capabilities remain available in the AGPL edition.
2. **Companies keep control of data:** Self-hosting is a first-class path.
3. **Privacy by design:** No personal data brokerage and no ads-based monetization.
4. **Sustainable, not extractive:** Revenue comes from enterprise value, not from exploiting user data.

---

## Value Proposition

### For Employees

- Find the right colleagues and internal opportunities faster
- Discover relevant groups, communities, and initiatives
- Use natural language instead of rigid keyword filters

### For Organizations

- Better internal mobility and staffing decisions
- Stronger visibility into skills and expertise
- Less dependency on fragmented tribal knowledge (chat, spreadsheets, inboxes)

---

## Sustainability and Revenue Model

PeopleMesh uses a transparent dual approach: open-source adoption plus paid enterprise value.

### 1) Open-Source Distribution (AGPL v3)

- Self-hosted community usage
- Core semantic search and matching
- Base integrations and local development workflows

### 2) Commercial Licensing (for organizations that need it)

- Alternative licensing for companies that cannot adopt AGPL obligations
- Advanced enterprise controls and governance features
- Optional private deployment terms for regulated environments

### 3) Enterprise Subscriptions and Services

- Managed hosting / operations support
- Priority support and SLAs
- Enterprise onboarding, migration, and integration services

---

## What PeopleMesh Monetizes vs. What It Does Not

### PeopleMesh monetizes

- Enterprise reliability, compliance, operations, and support
- Advanced capabilities required at scale
- Commercial terms for organizations with specific legal requirements

### PeopleMesh does not monetize

- Selling personal employee data
- Pay-for-visibility ranking that undermines relevance
- Ads-based targeting on user profile data

---

## Go-To-Market (Pragmatic Path)

1. Start with design partners solving internal discovery pain
2. Publish improvements in the open-source core
3. Convert organizations needing enterprise guarantees to paid plans
4. Expand through documented case studies and community credibility

---

## Summary

PeopleMesh is built as open infrastructure for internal discovery, with a business model based on enterprise-grade value: licensing flexibility, support, and operations.

This keeps the project aligned with its philosophy: open-source core innovation, user trust, and long-term sustainability.
